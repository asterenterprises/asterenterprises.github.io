Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Our commitment

Aster Enterprises, LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. Accessibility is a core discipline of our practice: we build products intended to be used by everyone, including people who rely on screen readers, keyboards, magnification, speech input, and other assistive technologies. We actively work to apply and improve upon the relevant accessibility standards across both this website and the products we build.

Conformance target

We aim for this website to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more usable for everyone.

Measures we take

  • Semantic HTML and ARIA where appropriate, so assistive technologies can interpret content reliably.
  • Keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements.
  • Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Color contrast evaluated against WCAG AA requirements.
  • Ongoing review of new pages and features before publishing.

Known limitations

Despite our efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. If you encounter a barrier, we want to hear from you — your feedback helps us prioritize improvements.

Feedback and contact

If you experience an accessibility issue with this website, or need content provided in an alternate format, please contact us. We aim to respond to accessibility requests within five business days.
