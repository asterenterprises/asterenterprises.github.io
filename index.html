Our Founder

Lawrence Weru

Founder & Principal

Lawrence Weru founded Aster Enterprises in 2014 to build software that helps mission-driven organizations deliver care to more people, more effectively. His work spans technical consulting, product design, engineering, and accessibility, grounded in research on how digital systems serve (or fail to serve) the people who use them.

  • Master of Science, Harvard Medical School (Dean's Scholar, 2023)
  • Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Science, Florida State University
  • TEDx speaker on design, civic data, and perception (2015)
  • Featured in Forbes, Harvard Gazette, Harvard Medicine News, Vox, Fast Company, Slate
  • Creator of OpenKeyNav, an open-source keyboard-navigation library
